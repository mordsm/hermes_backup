## Risks

- **Visibility risk:** cash withdrawals and `BIT` transfers can hide the true purpose of spending.
- **Classification risk:** some direct bank debits may be recurring bills, not discretionary spending.
- **Timing risk:** card statement windows and bank statement windows do not line up exactly.

## Recommendations

| Recommendation | Why | Risk if ignored |
|---|---|---|
| Separate **investing** from **spending** in your monthly review | prevents false alarms | healthy investing may look like overspending |
| Track **cash withdrawals** as a separate line | improves visibility | household spend can be undercounted |
| Split direct bank debits into **fixed bills** vs **one-offs** | shows the real baseline | you may overestimate flexible spending |
| Keep a monthly **family surplus target** | helps you protect savings | cashflow can look fine while spending drifts |
