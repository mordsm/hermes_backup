## 5) Risks / observations

- **Visibility risk:** cash withdrawals and `BIT` can hide the true purpose of spending.
- **Classification risk:** direct bank debits may include both fixed bills and discretionary spending.
- **Timing risk:** bank and card statement windows may not match perfectly.
- **Concentration risk:** if one card carries too much of the household spend, tracking gets harder.
